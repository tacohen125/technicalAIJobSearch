# Google Hiring Assessment — Practice Question Bank

Questions are organized by category. Each question is tagged with its type and any consistency-pair ID (questions sharing a pair ID test the same concept with different wording).

Format key:
- **[L]** = Likert statement (Strongly Disagree → Strongly Agree)
- **[SJ]** = Situational Judgment (select best/worst action)
- **{P##}** = Consistency pair ID

## Bonus: Nuance & Negation Questions

These test careful reading. Some are worded to flip expected responses.

### Q84 [L]
"I avoid giving direct feedback to colleagues unless they explicitly ask for it." *(Expected: Disagree — proactive feedback is valued)*

### Q85 [L]
"I do not need external validation to feel confident in my work." *(Nuance: Agree is fine, but Strong Agree could suggest resistance to feedback)*

### Q86 [L]
"I rarely change my mind once I've made a decision." *(Expected: Disagree — flexibility is valued)*

### Q87 [L]
"I avoid taking on tasks that are outside my job description." *(Expected: Strongly Disagree — initiative is valued)*

### Q88 [L]
"I prefer working independently over working with a team." *(Expected: Disagree — collaboration is valued, but nuance is acceptable)*

### Q89 [L]
"I tend to focus on what went wrong rather than what went right after completing a project." *(Expected: Disagree — balanced reflection is valued over negativity)*

### Q90 [L]
"I believe following rules is more important than achieving results." *(Nuance: Neither extreme is ideal — ethics matter, but so does thoughtful judgment)*
